Following the momentum of **EUDIW Unfold #1**, where we committed to organizing targeted sessions for key sectors, it became clear that the **banking and payments ecosystem** deserves dedicated attention.

- Gather feedback from financial institutions, payment service providers, and technical actors on implementation needs and priorities;  
- Share updates on technical readiness and ongoing pilots related to the financial sector.
