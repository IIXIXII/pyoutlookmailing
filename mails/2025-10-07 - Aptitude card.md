### About the Aptitude Member Card Experience  

This initiative is more than just a badge: itâ€™s the beginning of a **hands-on experiment** exploring the bridge between **physical credentials and digital credentials**.  

In collaboration with the **Imprimerie Nationale**, we will issue **physical consortium cards** that will serve as badges for all in-person meetings and events. These cards represent your **official status as a member of the Aptitude consortium**, and at the same time, they are part of a broader experiment that will evolve throughout the project.  

On the back of each card, you will find a **QR code built according to the ISO 22376:2023 standard**, containing both an **integrity proof** and an **authorship proof**.  
This same technology is already used in France for official credentials such as **boating licences**, which can be securely imported into the **France IdentitÃ©** wallet without relying on any centralised database.  

Similarly, for the **Aptitude Member Card**, there will be **no centralised database** for verifying membership rights.  
Over the next **24 months**, we will collectively explore how this credential can be **digitally issued and imported into various wallets**, testing new forms of **trust and interoperability** between the physical and digital worlds.  

This small but meaningful experiment will help us better understand how **physical titles and digital credentials** can complement and reinforce one another: an essential step in shaping the future of European digital identity.
